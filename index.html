About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Vigo. My research focuses on remote sensing, point clouds, compressive sensing, and 2D/3D sensors for high-precision mapping. I hold a joint PhD from the University of Vigo and the University of Salamanca, and a Master’s degree in Geomatics Engineering from the University of Stuttgart.
